Day 1

Tuna Salad

Tuna flakes, Romaine lettuce and, creamy mayonnaise layered in between 2 slices of wheat bread. A healthy and heavy lunch that’s packed with protein, omega-3 fatty acids, and carbohydrates that’s sure to fuel up anyone for a mountain hike!

Roast Beef Sandwich

In between two slices of wheat bread are strips of slow roasted beef, layered between fresh Romaine lettuce, tomato slices, and topped with a smoky barbecue sauce; a hearty meal to revitalize all those worn out muscles from the day’s hike!
